The Brand

Australian-based subtle luxury leather brand the kangaroo belt company is both an authentic mindset and emblem of modern luxury.

TKBC's fashion philosophy is to produce a framework for individuality by making accesories that are simple, functional and easily fit into the wearer's existing wardrobe.

Inspired by historical designs combined with Australian culture, Our handmade belts aspire to create authentic, timeless pieces that will stand the test of time.
