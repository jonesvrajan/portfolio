Saatva’s sleep wellnesS

Our mattress alleviates back pain and gets you a better night’s sleep.

  • Saatva’s patented spinal zone

    Saatva mattresses contain the patented ‘Spinal Zone' sleep technology that will help reduce back pain and stiffness through an active wire support in the center third of our mattress. The 'zone' helps provide correct spinal alignment resulting in increased sleep quality. Working in conjunction with Saatva's pocketed coil system, the Spinal Zone can react to any body type to provide a wide range of support, improve weight distribution, and lessen pressure points.

  • Proud to have the Chiropractic Seal of approval

    Saatva’s 'Spinal Zone' sleep technology is clinically proven to reduce back pain and has been recognized by the Congress of Chiropractic State Association (COCSA).

  • how Saatva benefits your sleep wellness

    • Densified foam layering system

      We pre-compress the cushioning in the lumbar area to eliminate breakdown and provide additional support. We also added a layer of memory foam to provide pressure relief in the areas commonly associated with pressure points.

    • Innerspring mattresses breathe

      Airflow between the springs allows for clean evaporation of water from nightly perspiration and keeps your body cool. This cleanliness acts as a significant deterrent to germs and dust mites.

    • Benefits of a luxury pillow top

      If you suffer from back, hip or overall joint pain you will feel more comfortable on a bed that contours to your body. Our euro top pillow top provides the perfect amount of contouring in conjunction with firm support for your spine.

  • Official mattress of the Health Bridge sleep clinic

    Saatva mattresses have been chosen the official mattress of the Healthbridge Sleep Clinic. Healthbridge is the northeast's premier sleep clinic helping people with sleep issues and is a clinical affiliate of Northshore/LIJ hospital.
