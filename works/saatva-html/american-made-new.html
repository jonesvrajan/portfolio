Proud to be 100% American Made

At Saatva we believe in creating jobs in America to furnish a better future!

  • American Handcrafted Quality

    At Saatva we choose to support our country's economy rather than outsourcing work overseas. The fact is, we are approached several times a year by foreign mattress manufacturers to build our products. They guarantee that the cost will be significantly less and our profits will be much higher. We turn down that offer in order to ensure a higher quality product, our mattresses are handcrafted and American builders do it beautifully.

  • Locally Sourced Materials

    Foams that are shipped from overseas are shrink wrapped and shipped moist, then they have to be off gassed which can have a very unpleasant odor. Our bio-based foams are sourced locally in the US and and are completely stable (no off gassing) before they even get to our factories. This ensures you get an odorless mattress the day it comes into your home.

  • Local Distribution

    Our American handcrafted mattresses have the best specifications offered by any company at the lowest price. Today, we have 9 independent factories building our proprietary product and 28 fulfillment centers delivering. We are responsible for the movement of hundreds of trucks weekly throughout every part of the country. On average our mattress travels less than 100 miles from our American factories to your home.

  • Keep more money in your pocket!

    Since we save so much on transportation and storefront costs incurred such as rent, commissions, utility costs and so much more, not only do we save you hundreds, we are also able to put the savings into materials and manufacturing. When you buy a Saatva mattress, you get the highest quality product and your money stays in America, enriching our economy!
