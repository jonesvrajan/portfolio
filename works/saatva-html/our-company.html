Our Story

Crafting a better mattress & the team that works hard to keep you happy!

Our Background

Our executive team has extensive experience in e-commerce and retail working in the past with companies like Facebook, Apple, Rosetta Stone, Men's Wearhouse, and Brookstone. At Saatva, we insist that all members of our team contribute to our highly collaborative work environment, which we believe is the genesis for creating a kind, happy, and productive organization. Rest assured, we will continue bending over backwards to bring you the best mattress value along with an easy and enjoyable buying experience. This attitude has helped Saatva become the best reviewed mattress brand on Google in 2012-2014.

WHO WE ARE

We are all over the place, in a good way! Our corporate headquarters are based out of Westport, CT. We work collaboratively with our marketing and Internet operation teams in Austin, TX, along with our offices in Norfolk, VA and New York. Together we provide an incomparable customer experience that eliminates the friction and confusion that are typically associated with mattress shopping. Saatva means Purity. We take our name seriously, so we offer the highest quality, eco-friendly mattress on the market. Without the middlemen.

THE SECRET TO SAATVA’S SUCCESS

Over the last several years, we have grown from a mid-sized regional luxury mattress company to one of the largest online-only mattress companies in America. Our non commissioned expert representatives give honest, no pressure guidance and we have built long term friendships with our delivery teams throughout our 30 fulfillment centers. Today, Saatva is comprised of 9 American factories and working with our team of professional online representatives, we are able to bring our environmentally friendly brand to consumers across all 50 states. We’re softies for the perfect mattress. Above all, we're a company that won't accept "Okay" as an answer to "How did you sleep?”. We love the culture that we've created and are proud to have a wonderfully diverse and spirited group of employees.
