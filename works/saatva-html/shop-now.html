  • Choosing the right mattress height

    When determining your Saatva mattress and foundation thickness options please consider the following points to ensure you are making the right choice.

    • 1. MEASURE Most importantly, always combine your visual thoughts with a tape measure, literally measuring your existing bed and/or measuring for the different heights we offer. This assures peace of mind with your Saatva purchase that you have ordered correctly.

    • 2. TRADITIONAL v. LOW PROFILE The Saatva 14.5" mattress and 11.5" were designed to feel identical in each of the comfort classifications. The same applies for our 8" and 4" foundations.

      3. FRAME When determining your bed height please account for the height of your 'metal frame' or 'bed frame' that the foundation and mattress will rest on.(Note, Saatva metal frames are 7" off the floor.)

      4. PERSONAL COMFORT Make sure that you will be comfortable getting in and out your bed based on the height you select.

      5. HEADBOARD/FOOTBOARD If you have a headboard and/or footboard, please be sure that you are happy with the amount of surface remaining of the

    • headboard and/or footboard based on the heights of the Saatva mattress and/or foundation you have carefully chosen.

      6. BEDSKIRT If you already have a bed skirt that you are planning on continuing to use, please make sure to measure from the floor to the top of the foundation to determine the size you will need. The most common bed skirts are approximately 14"/15" high, which would be fine with a 7" metal frame and a standard 8" foundation

      7. FURNITURE HEIGHT And last, when considering the proper total bed height, please also keep in mind aesthetic features such as the height of end tables and/ or windowsills in the room.
