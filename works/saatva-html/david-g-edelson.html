Saatva is proud to be the official mattress of the Healthbridge Sleep Clinic.

Dr. Edelson is the founder and medical director of HealthBridge, a nationally recognized weight loss and preventive health facility. Dr. Edelson has been a featured speaker on Obesity Management for Medical Grand Rounds at some of the top hospitals in the country and has published independent research in the Journal of the American Diabetes Association. He is a Fellow of the American College of Physicians, a Fellow of the American Society of Bariatric Physicians, as well as a Diplomat of the American Board of Bariatric Medicine.

Dr. Edelson has performed as a violinist at Carnegie Hall, ridden a bicycle from L.A. to Boston raising money for charity, and volunteers as a pilot for AngelFlight USA, an organization that transports sick patients in need of advanced treatment unavailable at their local healthcare facilities. He is also the author of an upcoming book of everyday tips to help people break through their personal barriers to weight loss.
